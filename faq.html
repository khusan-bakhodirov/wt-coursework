FAQ about cleaning services

How long does a standard cleaning take?

A standard cleaning service typically takes around 2 to 3 hours, depending on the size and condition of the space.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for both your home and the environment.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It is not necessary for you to be home during the cleaning service, but you can be if you prefer to supervise or provide instructions.

What should I do before the cleaning team arrives?

Please ensure that all personal items are cleared from surfaces to allow our team to clean efficiently. We will take care of everything else.

Is your cleaning service insured?

Yes, we are fully insured, giving you peace of mind that your home and belongings are safe with our professional cleaning team.

Can I book a recurring cleaning service?

Absolutely! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring cleaning services to suit your needs and schedule.

FAQ about our services

Do you offer one-time cleaning services?

Yes, we offer one-time cleaning services for customers who may need a deep clean or a one-off service for a special event.

Are your cleaners background checked?

Yes, all our cleaning professionals undergo thorough background checks to ensure trustworthiness and reliability.

What areas do you service?

We offer cleaning services across a wide range of areas, so please check our website or contact us to find out if we service your location.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, we provide all the cleaning supplies and equipment needed to complete the job. Just sit back and relax while we handle the rest!

What happens if I need to reschedule my cleaning?

If you need to reschedule, just let us know at least 24 hours in advance, and we will be happy to arrange another time that works for you.
